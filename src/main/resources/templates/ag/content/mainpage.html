Tech Solution for communities to eliminate wait times in utilizing community resources, to enable sharing within the community and to help members resell items.

Reserve

You can book facilities in advance using AllyGain platform, eliminating the need for you to wait for your turn to use any facility within your community, for example tennis court. After your community is registered on AllyGain and all the facilities are added by your community representative, you can start using this feature.

Share

Items such as step-ladders, hoses, tools and other utilities, and books are shared within the community. This feature makes sharing within the community easy. It allows residents to post items for sharing, borrow items, track status of items and to send reminders.

Buy & Sell

Reselling items within the community is very common. Buy & sell feature on this platform allows sellers to list items and buyers to view listed items for sale and contact sellers.

Register Community

To use the platform, first your community representative needs to register your community, upload data of community users who will use the platform, and add facilities & other details of the community.
